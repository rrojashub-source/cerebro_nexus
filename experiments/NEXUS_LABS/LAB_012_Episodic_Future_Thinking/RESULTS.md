## üéì Lessons Learned

### Design Decisions

1. **Constructive recombination > retrieval**
   - Tried: Pure pattern matching (predict from most similar episode)
   - Chose: Recombine multiple episodes into novel scenario
   - **Why:** More flexible, handles novel situations better

2. **Confidence calibration matters**
   - Balance: sample size + consistency
   - Too high confidence (always 90%+) = overconfident
   - Too low confidence (always 50%) = useless
   - Sweet spot: 50-90% range based on data quality

3. **Multi-option planning > single prediction**
   - Single future: "What will happen?"
   - Multiple futures: "Which is best?"
   - **Value:** Enables rational decision-making

### Challenges Overcome

**Challenge 1:** Generating coherent narratives from episode fragments
- **Solution:** Structured narrative template with historical basis + projected process + expected outcome
- **Result:** Readable, informative scenarios

**Challenge 2:** Handling scenarios with no historical precedent
- **Solution:** Return "unknown" prediction with 50% confidence, explicit "no data" reasoning
- **Result:** Graceful degradation for novel situations

**Challenge 3:** Ranking options when predictions are similar
- **Solution:** Confidence-weighted scoring: `confidence √ó success_probability`
- **Result:** Clear ranking even for close calls
